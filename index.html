Governance Cannot Only Exist Before Deployment

Most current approaches to AI and autonomous system safety focus on perception, navigation, capability, and post-incident investigation. They miss the execution-layer governance problem.

Authority, legitimacy, safety conditions, and operational justification must be validated while systems are actively running — not only at login, approval, or after a failure.
