## What This Is

The Zyzyx Authority System™ is a structured public governance and doctrine ecosystem for AI, robotics, and autonomous systems.

Most current approaches to AI and autonomous system safety focus on:
- Perception and navigation
- Capability and autonomy
- Post-incident investigation
- Regulatory checklists

**They miss the execution-layer governance problem.**

Authority, legitimacy, safety conditions, and operational justification must be validated **while systems are actively running** — not only at login, approval, or after a failure.

> *You cannot govern what you do not continuously validate. Authority is not a grant — it is a continuous validation.*
> — Zyzyx Authority System™

---

## Core Principle

**Humans set the why. Systems execute the how.**

Technology does not hold authority. It operates under human authority. Humans remain accountable. Always.

Governance cannot only exist:
- Before deployment
- At login
- During approval
- After a crash

It must exist **continuously during operation.**
